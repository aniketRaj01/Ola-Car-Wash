OLA CAR WASHtm

Connecting Cleaners

Ola Car Wash is a leading brand in the car cleaning segment and facilitating services across the country through its local service partners. Ola Car Wash basically connecting cleaners across the Country through its local service partners and extending the quality services at the customers’ doorstep.

Cleaning and hygiene work is a big issue at the social level, it is difficult to wash a car completely with a bucket of water, usually an average of 300 liters of water is wasted in washing or cleaning a car; however, we have made it possible to save more than 80% water in our projects. Water is precious; saving water is one of the major social responsibility, i.e. why we keep encouraging our service partners to save water.

Ola Car Wash has introduced door to door car washing & cleaning project in two categories, first one is door to door car washing bike project and another is door to door car washing e-cart project which is equipped with hi-end machinery that is capable to deliver car washing, cleaning, and interior dry cleaning, rubbing-polishing & sanitizing services at customers’ doorstep.

Our main objective is to change the mindset about car cleaning; interior cleaning is more important than exterior cleaning in the car cleaning segment, which is possible with the advance technologies, where a minimum amount of water is being used to clean a car at customer's doorstep.

USP: Empowering Local Partners to Create Job Opportunities for Local Cleaners under our Service Partnership Program.
